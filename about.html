About FloodFacts

FloodFacts is a student-led initiative focused on educating communities on flood preparedness, safety, and recovery.

Our Mission

We empower families and barangays with accessible resources to prepare for floods, respond safely, and rebuild stronger. By combining technology, research, and community stories, we aim to make flood knowledge practical and shareable.

What We Provide

  • Interactive preparedness checklists
  • Knowledge assessments through quizzes
  • Community-driven stories and recovery tips
  • Guidance for building resilience after floods

Technology Stack

This prototype is crafted with HTML5, Bootstrap 5, custom CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. Data persistence uses browser localStorage. Future phases will include a PHP + MySQL backend for user accounts and post-flood reports.
