Tips for Staying Ready

  • Review and update this checklist every season.
  • Practice evacuation drills with your household.
  • Store important documents both physically and digitally.
  • Monitor weather alerts and local government advisories.

Stay Informed

Identify reliable news channels, community alert systems, and weather apps. Knowing who to listen to and when to act makes all the difference.
