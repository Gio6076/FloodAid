Health & Safety

  • Avoid contact with flood water; disinfect cuts immediately.
  • Boil or treat water before drinking.
  • Wear protective gear when cleaning debris and mud.
  • Monitor local health advisories for outbreaks.

Recovery & Support

  • Document damage with photos for insurance or assistance.
  • Contact barangay or LGU offices for relief programs.
  • Disinfect surfaces with bleach solution and dispose of spoiled items.
  • Lean on community groups and shelters for temporary housing.
